PHOTOS TO DROP IN THIS FOLDER
=============================
The website is built to display these 4 images automatically. Just save your
photos into this "images" folder using these EXACT filenames (all lowercase):

1. headshot.jpg            -> your professional headshot (suit + blue tie)
2. golf-championship.jpg   -> you holding the OSAA state golf trophy
3. bucks-robot.jpg         -> the Bucks robotic dog (Raspberry Pi build)
4. ai-summit.jpg           -> you presenting on stage at the Marshall AI Summit

OPTIONAL:
5. dashboard-preview.jpg   -> a screenshot of the SANITIZED dashboard demo
                              (open stg-dashboard-demo.html, screenshot it).
                              If you skip this, the site links straight to the
                              live interactive demo instead.

Until a photo is added, the site shows a labeled placeholder in its spot —
nothing breaks. .png files work too; just rename to match (e.g. headshot.png
won't auto-load — keep the .jpg name, or tell me and I'll switch them).
